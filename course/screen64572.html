USE OF FUNDS

All applicable regulations and procedures regarding use of and accounting for government and other donor funds must be specifically followed under the guidance of NIDA-PAKISTAN Policy & Procedures as laid down. Any deviation from donor regulations or contractual terms, including emergency situations, shall be undertaken only after the written approval of the appropriate donor official. Any questions about these procedures or possible impropriety should be referred to the Chief Executive.
