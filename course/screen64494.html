7. Classification of Posts

Organizational hierarchy and minimum qualifications

7.1. Upper/Senior Management

Personnel providing leadership to Middle Management and sectoral or regional teams, such as Chief Executive Officer, Program Director, Operational Director, Managers/Coordinators, and Regional Heads.

Minimum Qualification: Master’s or Bachelor’s Degree in a relevant subject with 5–10 years of experience.

7.2. Middle Management

The second tier of sectoral staff providing professional backup to the Management Team, including Department/Unit Heads, Assistant Managers, Senior Officers, and Officers.

Minimum Qualification: Master’s or Bachelor’s Degree in a relevant subject with 2–5 years of experience.

7.3. Support Staff

Personnel providing support services to Middle Management, such as Secretaries, Computer Operators/Programmers, Assistants, Office Boys, Watchmen, Drivers, Maintenance Staff, Gardeners, Cleaners, Cooks, Sweepers, and Helpers.

Minimum Qualification: Specified in accordance with the post.
