Supplies and Utilities Documentation Process

The supplies and utilities management process ensures that materials and services are acquired, utilized, and documented in a transparent and accountable manner. The following key documents must be maintained to validate requisitions, expenditures, tax compliance, and the receipt of supplies.

  • Requisition Form (Approved): An authorized request submitted by the concerned department detailing the required supplies or utilities, including specifications, purpose, and estimated cost. Approval signifies authorization to proceed with procurement.
  • Float Clearance / Summary Sheet: A financial summary used to track expenses incurred under a float or advance, capturing itemized costs, receipts, and remaining balance. It ensures proper reconciliation and accountability of spent funds.
  • Float Invoice: The original invoice received from the vendor for the purchased supplies or utilities, serving as a financial and legal record for processing payment or clearing the advance.
  • Goods Received Notes (GRN): A document confirming that the ordered goods or utilities have been physically received, checked, and verified for quantity, quality, and compliance with the approved requisition.
  • Taxation (if applicable): Relevant tax documentation, including withholding tax, exemption certificates, or tax calculations, maintained to ensure compliance with statutory and donor regulations.
