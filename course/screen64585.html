Procurement Thresholds

NIDA-Pakistan applies defined procurement thresholds to ensure transparency, competitiveness, and value for money in all procurement activities.

Select a procurement threshold to view applicable procedures.

  • Low-Value Purchases (PKR 0 – 25,000)
  • Medium-Value Purchases (PKR 25,001 – 200,000)
  • High-Value Purchases (PKR 200,001 – 1,500,000)
  • National Tender Purchases (Above PKR 1,500,001)
