Finance Documentation

Vehicle Rental

The role of finance personnel is to ensure that the following documents are available before processing any vehicle rental payment request:

  • Requisition Form: Approval to initiate vehicle rental process.
  • Quotations: Price offers from multiple vendors for comparison.
  • Comparative Statements: Evaluation of quotations to justify selection.
  • Contracts: Signed rental agreement with terms and conditions.
  • Log Books / Fuel Consumption Report: Records of vehicle usage and fuel efficiency.
  • Vendor Invoices: Bills submitted by the rental service provider.
  • Taxation Documents: Relevant tax details or exemption certificates, if applicable.
  • Cross Cheque Photocopy: Copy of the payment instrument for records.
  • Acknowledgement on Letterhead: Confirmation of payment received from the vendor.
