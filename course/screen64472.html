1. Upholding the Highest Standards of Personal and Professional Conduct

Employees, volunteers, and contributors of NIDA-Pakistan are expected to act in full alignment with the organisation’s mission, values, and strategic objectives. Individual conduct should actively strengthen NIDA-Pakistan’s credibility, performance, and impact.

All representatives must ensure that their behaviour—during and outside official working hours—enhances the organisation’s reputation and never undermines public trust. Everyone is required to treat all people as equals, with respect, dignity, and cultural sensitivity, and to ensure that all communications and publications portray individuals respectfully.

While on duty, personnel should dress appropriately for their role and operating context, projecting a professional image of NIDA-Pakistan. These standards apply equally to online conduct, including social media and other digital platforms.
