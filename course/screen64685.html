EO 13522

Executive Order 13522, signed by President Obama on December 9th, 2009, calls for Federal agencies to work together with employees and unions to improve the delivery of high quality services to the American people.  It is essential that you have a basic understanding of EO 13522.

This Executive Order:

  • Creates the National Council on Federal Labor-Management Relations comprised of high level Federal management and union representatives.
  • Instructs agency heads to establish labor-management forums at the level of recognition and other appropriate levels within their agencies to help identify problems and propose solutions to better serve the public and agency missions.
  • Requires agencies to submit written implementation plans for Council approval.
  • Instructs management to allow employees and their union representatives to have pre-decisional involvement in all workplace matters to the fullest extent practicable.
