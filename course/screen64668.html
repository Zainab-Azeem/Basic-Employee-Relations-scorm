Definition of Sexual Harassment

Sexual harassment may occur in multiple forms, including:

  • Verbal: Slurs, derogatory comments, inappropriate jokes.
  • Visual: Derogatory posters, gestures, intimidating looks.
  • Physical: Unwanted touching, assault, or presence in work area.
  • Digital/Online: Inappropriate emails, social media, or internet content.
