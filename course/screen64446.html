Knowledge Reviews

Throughout this course there are "Knowledge Review" screens where you must respond to questions based on the content you have learned.

When you select a response, you must then select the "I'm Done" button to check your answers and receive feedback.

Correct responses are marked with a checkmark.

Incorrect responses are marked with an X. If you select an incorrect response, you can select the "Show Me" button and checkmarks will display next to the correct responses.
