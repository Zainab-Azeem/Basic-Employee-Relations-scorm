Communication Documentation Requirements

The communication-related documentation ensures regulated usage of organizational communication resources, compliance with approved policies, and accurate financial record-keeping. The following documents are essential to verify authorization, expenditures, and proper utilization of communication services.

  • Approved Communication Policy: An officially endorsed policy outlining guidelines, permissible usage, responsibilities, and cost control measures for organizational communication tools and services such as telephones, internet, and mailing.
  • Approved Expense Report: A documented and approved summary of costs incurred on communication services, including expense justifications, supporting bills, and payment approvals to ensure transparency and financial accountability.
  • Calling Cards / Post-paid Bill: Official billing records or post-paid communication invoices, including calling cards, mobile data usage, or telephone bills, used for reconciling expenses and verifying compliance with approved communication policy.
