POLICY STATEMENT

Procurement of the required quality and quantity of goods and services at the required time and place and in the most cost-effective and efficient manner to provide support to the operations and other related persons. Affirmative action will be taken to provide maximum practical opportunity to local supplier & businesses, to participate as suppliers and contractors in the acquisition of goods and services by NIDA-PAKISTAN. Those responsible for procurement shall continuously conduct searches of the market for new sources of supply. Any form of discrimination is prohibited in the purchasing processes. NIDA-PAKISTAN offices Logistics department or designated personnel will purchase all goods and services on the best terms consistent with the required quality and delivery, and at the lowest total cost. Acquisition will be without favoritism and on a competitive basis, whenever practical, to obtain maximum value for each amount spent. All interested suppliers shall receive fair and impartial consideration.
