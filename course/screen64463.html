Disaster Risk Reduction:
While it is next to impossible to stop the natural and human induced disasters and calamities from happening, the impact of these can be significantly reduced to minimize losses through effective Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) practices. NIDA's strategic objective focuses on strengthening these practices across all stages – preparedness, response, and recovery – to minimize the devastation caused by natural calamities in Pakistan.
