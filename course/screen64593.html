Purchase Committee Composition

The Purchase Committee at NIDA-Pakistan is constituted to ensure transparency, impartiality, and technical soundness in procurement decisions. The committee operates in accordance with approved procurement policies and authorization limits.

Core Committee Members

  • Finance / Grants Representative – Ensures budget availability, financial compliance, and donor alignment.
  • Admin / Operations Representative – Oversees procedural compliance, logistics, and documentation.
  • Program Representative – Confirms programmatic relevance, timelines, and beneficiary impact.
  • Technical / Subject Specialist – Assesses technical specifications, quality standards, and suitability.

Role of Chief Executive

  • May participate in high-value, sensitive, or complex procurements.
  • Provides strategic oversight and final approval where required.
  • Ensures organizational risk management and compliance.

Committee Responsibilities

  • Review and evaluate bids objectively.
  • Ensure value for money and competitive selection.
  • Document decisions and recommendations.
  • Maintain transparency and audit readiness.
