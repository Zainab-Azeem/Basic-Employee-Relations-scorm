Delegation of Authority

Chief Executive/Program Director can delegate all or any of his/her powers to any person. However, such delegation of powers shall be made in writing.

The following is the authorization limit, and the personnel authorized to approve the expenses/payments up to the specified limits are as follows:

Approval Limit (PKR) Authorized Personnel
01 – 100,000 Project Manager
100,001 – 500,000 Program Manager, Operations Manager
500,001 – 1,000,000 Director Program
1,000,001 and above Chief Executive

In regional offices, if the Chief Executive is not available, the Director Program can only approve expenses/payments up to PKR 2,000,000.
