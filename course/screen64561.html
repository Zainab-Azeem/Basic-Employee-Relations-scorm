Objectives

This lesson teaches you the fundamentals of logistics and procurement management. Upon completion of this lesson, you will:

  • Know the key logistics assessment components and how they guide operational planning, including transport, warehousing, availability of goods, pricing, regulations, and coordination with partners.
  • Understand the link between logistics and program objectives, including how needs are defined, feasibility is evaluated, and logistics plans and budgets are developed.
  • Understand the procurement process and its steps, including requisition, quotation, competitive bidding, bid analysis, purchasing, delivery, and documentation.
  • Know how to apply the “five rights” of logistics — the right goods, in the right quantity, at the right quality, at the right time, and at the right price — to ensure successful operations.
  • Understand special considerations in procurement, including donor compliance, fraud prevention, transparency, and the principles of fair competition and best value for money.
