Prohibited Uses of Official Time

Certain activities are categorized by the Statute in Section 7131(b) as internal union business, and, therefore, union representatives are not entitled to "official time" when engaged in these activities. For example, activities such as solicitation of membership, union elections, and collection of dues, must be done on non-duty time.
