Safety and Security

Security risks include violence, crime, and war. Safety risks include accidents, disease, and health hazards.

  • Staff must take reasonable precautions to prevent accidents.
  • Unsafe behavior by one member can endanger the entire team.
  • Regular safety checks and risk assessments are required.

Example: Wearing seatbelts, using PPE, and following travel protocols reduce exposure to risk.
