ER specialist’s Role in Contract Administration

Again, while the ER specialist may or may not have a direct role in contract administration, you should be prepared to assist agency supervisors and management with questions that may arise. As such, having a firm understanding of the requirements under the Statute and under the CBA will be important to the ER or ELR Specialist’s success. Also, feel free to consult with your Labor Relations Specialist when in doubt about the meaning of a provision contained in the CBA.

The key point here is that you are helping to set the labor relations atmosphere for the agency. Solid labor-management relationships will contribute to the accomplishment of the agency’s mission and goals.
