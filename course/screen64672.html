What Are Not Formal Discussions

The following list identifies types of meetings that are generally not considered to be "formal discussions" under the Statute. However, please check the applicable CBA as the agency may have agreed to follow other procedures related to these types of meetings. 

  • Discussions on individual work assignments
  • Performance appraisals 
  • Progress reviews
  • Performance counseling meetings
  • Conduct counseling meetings
