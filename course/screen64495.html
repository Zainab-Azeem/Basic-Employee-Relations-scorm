8. Recruitment and Selection

NIDA-Pak is committed to ensuring transparent, equitable, and merit-based recruitment and selection processes that promote fairness, inclusivity, and diversity. The following standards and procedures shall govern all hiring activities across the organization.

8.1. Equal Employment Opportunity and Non-Discrimination

NIDA-Pak is an equal opportunity employer. All employment decisions are based on merit, competencies, and organizational needs. Discrimination on the basis of gender, disability, age, ethnicity, religion, or any other status is strictly prohibited.

  • Reasonable accommodations will be made for individuals with disabilities, provided this does not create undue hardship for the organization.
  • In cases where male and female candidates achieve equal assessment scores, preference will be given to female candidates where the role is feasible, as part of NIDA-Pakâ€™s commitment to gender equity.
  • Employees may raise concerns related to workplace discrimination directly with their supervisor, the HR focal person, or the Chief Executive Officer, without fear of reprisal. Breaches of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including termination.

8.2. Recruitment Advertisement

Vacant positions are announced publicly using cost-effective and targeted channels such as electronic, print, and online platforms preferably.

Job descriptions must clearly state:

  • Minimum qualifications and experience,
  • Application process and deadline,
  • Equal opportunity statement.

Internal candidates who meet the eligibility requirements may apply and will be considered on equal terms. Prior experience with NIDA-Pak will be valued but does not guarantee appointment.
