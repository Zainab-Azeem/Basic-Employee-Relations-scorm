Objectives

This lesson provides a basic overview of key labor relations concepts for ER Specialists who may be new or have had less than three years of on-the-job experience in a unionized working environment. We will discuss:

  • ER Specialist’s role and the supervisor’s role in labor relations
  • Basic rights for employees, unions, and management under the Federal Service Labor-Management Relations Statute
  • Important labor relations concepts
  • Collective bargaining agreements and ER Specialist’s role and the supervisor’s role in contract administration
  • Unfair labor practices 
  • Executive Order 13522
