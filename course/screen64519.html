19. Performance Evaluation

Since its inception, NIDA-Pak has been establishing various promotion systems and increment procedures that are upgraded with the passage of time.

20. Performance Appraisal

The appraisal is carried out for employees who have completed a minimum of six months of employment (from joining date till 30th June). Based on their performance, increments are awarded, and it may also result in promotions. Before granting an official promotion, the employee must pass through an interview to ensure that they possess the capabilities to carry out the job and fulfill the assigned responsibilities.

Conditions may vary for temporary or contract employees. Project staff is eligible for performance evaluation if the project duration is at least one year.

21. Compliance with Regulatory Requirements

All employees must comply with the rules, regulations, and policies of NIDA-Pak. If an employment relationship is established, both the employee and NIDA-Pak reserve the right to terminate employment at any time and for any reason.

NIDA-Pak staff rules do not constitute an employment contract between NIDA-Pak and its employees. NIDA-Pak reserves the right to modify its policies at any time, without prior notice. This document supersedes any previous memos or notices.

NIDA-Pak ensures that its policies and procedures are always aligned with applicable labor laws, taxation rules, and other governmental regulations.
