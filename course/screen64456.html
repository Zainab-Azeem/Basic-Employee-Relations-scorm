Improved Community-based Health Care Services:

NIDA aims to increase access to quality health care services in underserved communities. NIDA will work with local authorities to assess existing healthcare infrastructure, identifying gaps in service delivery and capacity of health facilities particularly in isolated and remote areas. This will provide a clear picture of where resources are most needed. NIDA will then partner with government health departments to revitalize existing health centers in remote areas. This includes equipping them with the necessary tools and technology to deliver quality services.
