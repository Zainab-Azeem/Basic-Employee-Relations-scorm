Request for Quotation (RFQ)

RFQs must include:

  • Reference to original requisition
  • Originator and supplier addresses
  • Preamble explaining need
  • Quantity, full specifications, and delivery requirements
  • Means of transport, packing, marking (visibility policy), currency, and payment terms
  • Quotation submission deadline and validity
