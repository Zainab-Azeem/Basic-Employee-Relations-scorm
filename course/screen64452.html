Brief Organization Profile

NIDA-Pakistan stands for National Integrated Development Association, evolved at the grassroots level. Initially, NIDA began its journey as a leading Community-Based Organization (CBO) under the name of Kohistan Development & Environmental Council (KDEC) back in 1998. Being at the forefront and locally rooted, NIDA-Pakistan management envisioned expanding its scope and services. NIDA-Pakistan formally registered itself with the Social Welfare Department KP (previously NWFP) and later, in 2008, as a registered NGO with registration number is 611/5/5650 under the Society Registration Act of 1860. The organization aims to empower vulnerable communities across Pakistan, including Azad Jammu and Kashmir (AJK) and Gilgit-Baltistan (GB).

Our Vision statement

“A self-reliant, vibrant, and harmonious society, characterized by prosperity, peace, pluralism, and resilience in geographies served by NIDA.”

Our Mission statement

“To catalyze holistic and community-driven sustainable development by building local human capabilities and leveraging multi-sectoral holistic programming through building partnerships.”
