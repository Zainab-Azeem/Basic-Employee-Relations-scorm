House Rent Documentation Requirements

The following documents are required for processing house rent-related agreements. These documents ensure legal compliance, transparency, and proper financial documentation in accordance with organizational and taxation requirements.

  • Contract: A duly signed rental agreement between the tenant and the landlord, outlining the terms, conditions, rental amount, and duration of the tenancy.
  • Copy of ID Card: A valid photocopy of the landlordâ€™s and/or tenant's national identity card for verification and record-keeping purposes.
  • Cross Cheque Photo Copy: A photocopy of the cross cheque issued for rent payment to ensure traceability, financial accountability, and proper audit trail.
  • Taxation (Exemption): Relevant documents related to tax deduction or exemption, including exemption certificates or tax withholding statements, as applicable under local regulations.
  • Acknowledgment: A formal acknowledgment or receipt issued by the landlord confirming payment of rent, serving as evidence for financial reporting and audit compliance.
