Objectives

The purpose of the NIDA-Pakistan Security Policy is to ensure that all staff operate in the safest possible manner while performing their duties, including in potentially life-threatening situations.

  • Define safety and security responsibilities for staff and management.
  • Provide guidance on acceptable risk and operational conduct.
  • Ensure staff understand evacuation, crisis, and security protocols.
