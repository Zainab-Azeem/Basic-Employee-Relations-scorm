Purpose and Course Modules

The purpose of this course is to improve the effectiveness of entry-level staff by developing their fundamental skills and the necessary understanding on the  NIDA-Pakistan.

In order to obtain these skills and understanding this course will cover the following:

  • Module 1:Introduction & Orientation of NIDA-Pakistan
  • Module 2:Code of Conduct
  • Module 3: HR Policy
  • Module 4: Financial Management Policy
  • Module 5: Procurement Policy
  • Module 6: Security Policy
  • Module 7: Safeguarding Policy
  • Final Assessment
