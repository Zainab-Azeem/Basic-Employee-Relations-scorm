Vehicle Fuel Charges

Before processing any fuel charge payments, finance personnel must ensure that the following supporting documents are complete and attached:

  • Requisition: Approved request for fuel usage and payments.
  • Valid Contract for Fuel Supply: Signed agreement with authorized fuel provider.
  • Invoices of Fuel: Original fuel invoices issued by the supplier.
  • Photocopies of Log Books: Verified vehicle usage records.
  • Fuel Intake Report: Documentation of fuel quantities consumed per vehicle.
  • Cross Cheque Photocopy: Copy of cheque issued for payment.
  • Acknowledgment on Letterhead: Written confirmation of payment received from the supplier.
