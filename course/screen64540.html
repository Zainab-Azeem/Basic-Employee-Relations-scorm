To ensure transparency, accountability, and compliance in financial management, all essential documents must be maintained in each financial file. Staff should adhere to the following requirements:

  • Invoice Calculations: Ensure accurate calculation of each invoice (avoid alterations or cuttings).
  • Budget Availability: Verify that funds are available under the approved budget.
  • Budget Codes: Use proper account and budget codes for each transaction.
  • Authorization: Obtain necessary approvals and signatures before processing payments.
  • Supporting Documents: Attach all relevant supporting documentation to substantiate expenses.
  • Legal Obligations: Ensure compliance with all legal and contractual requirements.
  • Operational Expenses: Payments up to PKR 20,000 can be made in cash; amounts above this must be issued through cross cheque.
  • Payment Vouchers: Complete with correct codes, taxation details, amounts, and authorized signatures.
  • Paid Stamp: Affix a "PAID" stamp on every settled invoice.
  • Cheque Photocopies: Keep copies of all issued cheques for records.
  • Acknowledgement: Secure acknowledgement of payment on official letterheads from vendors or recipients.
