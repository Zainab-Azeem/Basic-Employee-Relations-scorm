18.2 Dearness Allowance

Dearness Allowance is an allowance which covers the extra daily expenditure incurred by an employee in consequence of duty away from the Headquarter but not within the District where his duty station/headquarter is located. A travel request form should be signed by the Line Manager and shared with the Admin/Fin department. When a member of staff travels on official duty, reasonable out-of-pocket expenses are allowed besides the travel cost.

  • The location for stay should be the safest, economical and efficient means.
  • While on a visit to any of the NIDA-Pak offices and any out-of-town visit, the boarding and lodging should be within the respective field office/out-of-town location. In such cases, only the Per Diem rate elaborated for the following designations shall apply:
S.N Designation Rate of Per Diem (Ordinary) Rate of Per Diem (Special) Remarks
1 Board of Directors / CEO 8,000/- 12,000/-
2 Senior Management 6,000/- 10,000/- Directors / Coordinators & Managers
3 Officers 3,000/- 5,000/-
4 Support Staff 2,000/- 4,000/-
  • Upon return, the staff must submit the Travel Expense Sheet and reimburse the expenses.

NOTE: The special rates of DA are applicable in respect of official visits to Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore, Karachi, Quetta, AJK, Gilgit Baltistan, and Gwadar.

In KP Province, Special Rate of DA shall be admissible for official visits to Chitral, D.I. Khan, Kohistan, Buner, Swat, and Shangla Districts (for staff stationed at the Headquarter Besham and Provincial Headquarter Peshawar).

18.2.7 Original vouchers/receipts must be provided for reimbursement of expenditures. Above rates will be applicable when visit abroad is financially supported from NIDA-Pakistan’s own budget.

18.2.8 In case a visit, tour, meeting or training abroad is sponsored by a project donor or any other Organization/Agency, they will be responsible for making payments as per their Rules and Authorization.

18.2.9 The day of travel shall be considered for full Dearness Allowance (DA). For other days, the daily expenses shall be allocated as follows:
– Breakfast: 10%
– Lunch: 45%
– Dinner: 45%
In cases where breakfast is provided complimentary or lunch is included, the DA shall be proportionally adjusted to reflect such deductions.

18.2.10 When full board is provided, no DA shall be paid if the travel is funded from NIDA-Pakistan’s own resources. For travel funded by donors, DA shall be disbursed in accordance with the respective donor’s policy.
