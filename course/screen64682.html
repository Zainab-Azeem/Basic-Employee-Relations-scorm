Unfair Labor Practices or ULPs

An alleged violation of a right protected by 5 U.S.C. Chapter 71 (the Federal Service Labor-Management Relations Statute).

Unfair Labor Practices (ULPs) ensure that the rights of management, unions, and employees guaranteed by law are applied correctly; they can be filed by any person.

The Statute contains a provision, 5 U.S.C. 7116, which formally defines the types of unfair labor practices that can be committed by agencies and unions.
