ULP Process

The ULP process includes:

  • Charges filed with the Federal Labor Relations Authority (FLRA)
  • Investigation by FLRA
  • Settlement efforts
  • Either dismissal, or else complaint and hearing before an Administrative Law Judge
  • ALJ Decision
May be "appealed" to FLRA or subsequently to the federal courts, where applicable.

Consult the FLRA website for more detailed information about the ULP process.
