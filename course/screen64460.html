Water Supply, Sanitation and Hygiene:
NIDA's strategic objective is to address this gap and empower communities to achieve a healthier future. NIDA will work closely with relevant government departments and local communities to explore various options. This could involve constructing new and repairing existing water infrastructure, drilling new wells or tube-wells, installing water supply pipelines in water-scarce regions, or introducing rainwater harvesting systems. Similarly, NIDA will support the construction and improvement of sanitation facilities, prioritizing user safety and environmental sustainability. NIDA will also launch comprehensive hygiene promotion campaigns, educating communities about the importance of hand-washing, safe water handling, and proper sanitation practices.
