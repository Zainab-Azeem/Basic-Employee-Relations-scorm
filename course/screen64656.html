Bargaining Units Exclusions

The Statute at ยง7112 excludes certain types of positions from being included in collective bargaining units. Exclusions include:

  • Supervisors
  • Management officials
  • Confidential employees
  • Both professional employees and other employees in the same unit, unless a majority of professional employees vote for inclusion in the unit
  • Employees engaged in:
    • Personnel work other than purely clerical capacity
    • Administering provisions of the Statute
    • Intelligence, counterintelligence, investigative, or security work that directly affects national security
  • Employees primarily engaged (spending 50 percent or more of their time) in investigations or audit functions relating to the work of individuals whose duties directly affect the internal security of the agency, but only if the functions are undertaken to ensure that the duties are discharged with honesty and integrity.
