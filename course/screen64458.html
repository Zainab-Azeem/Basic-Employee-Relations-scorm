Food and Nutrition Security:

NIDA-Pakistan has recently adopted Food & Nutrition Security as one of the core areas. NIDA-Pakistan is currently implementing FNS sensitive programs in KP with the support of WHH. NIDA-Pakistan, building on its FNS expertise, is expanding its portfolio further to implement project with focus on food systems which is an emerging thematic area to reduce poverty and achieve zero hunger (SDG 1 and 2). NIDAPakistan will continue with FNS programming approach in the future, which shall be reflected as core thematic area.
