Popup Blocker

Some versions of Internet Explorer have a feature called a popup blocker. There are files throughout this course which the popup blocker may not allow to function properly.

To turn off Internet Explorer's popup blocker:
  • At the top of the browser click on "Tools."
  • Scroll down to "Popup Blocker."
  • Click on "Turn Off Popup Blocker."

Once you exit the course, repeat the above steps to turn the popup blocker on.
