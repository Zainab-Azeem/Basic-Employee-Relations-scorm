7. Zero Tolerance for Sexual Violence, Exploitation, and Harassment

NIDA-Pakistan is committed to a safe, respectful, and professional environment where any abuse of power is actively prevented. All employees, volunteers, partners, and contributors are strictly prohibited from engaging in any form of sexual violence, including sexual exploitation, sexual abuse, and sexual harassment.

Prohibited Conduct (non-exhaustive)

  • Sexual exploitation: abusing a position of vulnerability, differential power, or trust for sexual purposes, including gaining monetary, social, or political benefit.
  • Sexual abuse: actual or threatened physical intrusion of a sexual nature, by force, coercion, or under unequal or exploitative conditions.
  • Sexual harassment: any unwanted verbal, non-verbal, or physical conduct of a sexual nature that undermines dignity or creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment.
  • Misuse of positional power or working relationships to solicit sexual favours.
  • Any exchange of money, goods, services, or other benefits in return for sexual services.
  • Sexually suggestive remarks, unwelcome touching or proximity, sharing/display of suggestive or explicit material (including via email, messaging apps, or social media), and sexist conversations or jokes—whether verbal, written, visual, or non-verbal.

These prohibitions apply both within and outside the workplace whenever a clear link exists between the individual’s actions and their role in NIDA-Pakistan. Breaches will result in serious disciplinary action, which may include termination, legal proceedings, and reporting to relevant authorities.
