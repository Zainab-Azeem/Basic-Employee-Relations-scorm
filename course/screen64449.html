Course Completion Requirements

Length of Course and Commitment:

A commitment on behalf of the employee/staff is required in order to successfully complete this 4.5 hour course. The course materials include a variety of readings, knowledge checks, review questions, and a final assessment.

In order to pass the course and obtain your certificate, a minimum of 70% accuracy must be obtained on the final assessment.
