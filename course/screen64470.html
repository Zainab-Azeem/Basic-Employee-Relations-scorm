SCOPE AND POLICIES

The NIDA-Pakistan Code of Conduct and its accompanying policies apply to all relevant persons and entities associated with NIDA-Pakistan across all locations of operation.

Applicability

  1. All NIDA-Pakistan employees, irrespective of contract type (permanent, temporary, interns, and seconded personnel), scope of responsibilities, or duty station.
  2. Employees, executives, and governing body members of partner organisations receiving material or non-material support from NIDA-Pakistan.
  3. Employees, executives, and governing body members of affiliated social enterprises in which NIDA-Pakistan holds a share or partnership interest.
  4. Consultants and freelancers engaged by NIDA-Pakistan under contractual agreements.
  5. Volunteers contributing time or expertise to NIDA-Pakistan activities (e.g., advisory committees, community action groups, campaign teams).
  6. Suppliers and service providers working with NIDA-Pakistan.

Members of NIDA-Pakistan’s governing bodies—including the General Assembly, Executive Board, and any appointed committees—are equally bound to uphold the Code of Conduct.

For the purposes of this Code, the groups listed in points b through f are hereafter collectively referred to as Contributors.
