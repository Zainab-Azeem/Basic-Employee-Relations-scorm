Primacy to Life

While staff are expected to exercise care in protecting NIDA-Pakistan assets, their personal safety always comes first.

  • Do not risk life to protect property.
  • Follow security protocols even if it limits access to assets.
  • Use judgment in emergencies to prioritize human life.

Note: Staff safety takes precedence over all operational tasks.
