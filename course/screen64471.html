NIDA-Pakistan Rules of Conduct

NIDA-Pakistan expects all employees and contributors to comply with applicable national and international laws and to fully adhere to this Code of Conduct. Compliance extends beyond the workplace and outside official working hours whenever an identifiable link exists to NIDA-Pakistan, its work, or its reputation.

In situations of uncertainty or conflicting instructions, legal compliance takes precedence over any other directive, including those from management.
