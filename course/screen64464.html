Role of Third Parties:

NIDA will support affected communities in implementing swift and effective emergency response actions to save lives, provide immediate relief and ensure the safety of affected people during and after the disasters. In the aftermath of a disaster, NIDA will support recovery efforts by providing immediate assistance to affected communities. This could involve temporary housing solutions, food distribution, and psychosocial support services. NIDA will involve local communities and relevant Government organizations to uundertake long-term reconstruction efforts focussed on rebuilding communities, restoring livelihoods, reconstruction infrastructure and creating resilient systems to mitigate future risks from disasters.
