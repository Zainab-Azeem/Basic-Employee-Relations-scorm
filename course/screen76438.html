Organogram

The organogram of NIDA-Pakistan represents a structured governance and operational framework that supports strategic leadership, accountability, and effective program delivery. The Board of Directors provides policy direction and institutional oversight, supported by the Chief Executive, Internal Auditor, and Governance & Leadership unit—all ensuring compliance, transparency, and strategic alignment. Reporting to the Chief Executive, seven core directorates—Program, Finance, MEAL, HR & Administration, Operations & Logistics, Advocacy & Communication, and Fundraising & Resource Mobilization—work cohesively to ensure systematic planning, implementation, financial integrity, monitoring, and stakeholder engagement. This structure enables efficient coordination, result-based management, and donor confidence while promoting innovation, community responsiveness, and sustainability. Clear roles, accountability mechanisms, and functional specialization help streamline decision-making, strengthen institutional performance, and enhance NIDA-Pakistan’s capacity to implement development, humanitarian, and resilience-building programs with professionalism and impact.
