Preserving Local Cultural Heritage:

NIDA aims to Safeguard and celebrate local rich cultural heritage aims to preserve, appreciate, and empower these vibrant traditions. Among a host of interventions, NIDA will particularly focus to build capacity of local artisans through skill development training and workshops, honing their craft and fostering innovation. Support will be provided to bridge the gap between artisans and markets, creating linkages that translate cultural expression into economic opportunity. Technical and financial assistance will help the artisans develop marketable products and participate in cultural festivals, showcasing their talents on a wider stage.
