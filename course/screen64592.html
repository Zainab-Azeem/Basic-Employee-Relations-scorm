Roles and Responsibilities

Clear definition of roles and responsibilities is essential to ensure accountability, segregation of duties, and transparency in the procurement process at NIDA-Pakistan. Each function contributes to effective controls and compliance with organizational and donor requirements.

Logistics / Procurement Unit

  • Manages the end-to-end procurement process.
  • Issues Requests for Quotations (RFQs) and tenders.
  • Coordinates bid opening, evaluation, and documentation.
  • Ensures compliance with procurement policies and thresholds.

Requesting Departments / Projects

  • Identify procurement needs and raise Purchase Requisitions.
  • Provide clear specifications, quantities, and delivery timelines.
  • Confirm receipt and satisfactory use of goods or services.
  • Ensure requests align with approved budgets and work plans.

Finance Department

  • Verifies availability of budget before procurement.
  • Processes supplier payments upon completion of documentation.
  • Ensures tax compliance and proper financial coding.
  • Maintains financial records for audit and reporting.

Purchase Committee

  • Evaluates bids and quotations objectively.
  • Ensures fairness, competition, and value for money.
  • Recommends suppliers for approval.
  • Documents decisions for transparency and audit trail.
