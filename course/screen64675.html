The representative is entitled to:

  • Actively participate, and to clarify the issues or facts.
  • Assist the employee in providing favorable or relevant facts.

The representative is NOT entitled to:

  • Answer questions for the employee.
  • Break up or disrupt the meeting.  Prevent you from carrying out your investigation of the matter.
