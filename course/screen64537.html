HR Policy Acknowledgement

The present HR Policy of NIDA-Pak is considered read and understood by the employee. The English version is also translated and available in Urdu. A copy of this Policy is signed by the employee and kept as record in the employee file.

The employee will sign the record of signature regarding this document. Each employee may consult freely the present HR Policy which is available at any time in the administration office.

This HR Policy takes effect for an undetermined period from 01/01/2025.

The HR Policy forms part of the employment contract.
