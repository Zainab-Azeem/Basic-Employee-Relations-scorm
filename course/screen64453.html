NIDA Pakistan Core Values

NIDA-Pakistan upholds the following core values:

1. Humanity: Placing human dignity and compassion at the center of all interventions, ensuring that our work alleviates suffering and promotes well-being.

2. Impartiality: Serving communities without discrimination on the basis of ethnicity, religion, gender, or political affiliation, and prioritizing those in greatest need.

3. Volunteerism: Promoting community participation and encouraging selfless contributions of time, skills, and resources for the common good.

4. Neutrality: Maintaining an unbiased stance in political, religious, and ideological matters to ensure trust and access to all communities.

5. Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty, ethical conduct, and moral principles in every aspect of our work.

6. Accountability: Taking full responsibility for our actions and decisions, ensuring that resources are used effectively for their intended purpose.

7. Transparency: Operating in an open and clear manner, sharing information with stakeholders, donors, and communities to foster trust and collaboration.
