OBJECTIVES

As an organisation engaged in development cooperation and humanitarian assistance, NIDA-Pakistan is accountable both to the communities it serves and to its donors — including individuals, businesses, foundations, and public institutions in Pakistan and abroad. Our reputation depends on how we fulfil these responsibilities, making ethical and professional conduct essential for every employee, volunteer, and partner.

The Code of Conduct aims to:

  • Define NIDA-Pakistan’s shared values and conduct standards.
  • Ensure consistent, ethical behaviour across all staff, volunteers, and partners.s
  • Clarify expectations for professional conduct.
  • Inform stakeholders of the standards they can expect from NIDA-Pakistan representatives.
  • Present contractual and ethical obligations in clear terms.
