Human Resources — Salaries

Each employee’s personal file must contain the following essential documents to ensure proper HR record-keeping, transparency, and compliance:

  • Personal Requisition Form (PRF): Approval document for initiating staff recruitment.
  • Advertisement / Head Hunting Policy (if any): Record of recruitment process and transparency measures.
  • Staff Hiring Notes: Documentation of selection decisions and approvals.
  • Contract / ID Card / Acceptance: Signed employment contract, staff ID, and acceptance letter.
  • Degrees, Certificates & Experience Letters: Verified academic and professional credentials.
  • Payroll Sheet / Payment Voucher / Taxes: Salary processing details including tax deductions.
  • Copies of Cross Cheque / Fund Transfer: Evidence of salary disbursement.
  • Salary Slips: Monthly salary statements issued to the employee.
