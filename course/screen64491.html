Purpose of this policy

The main purpose of this Policy is to maintain an effective system of internal control in order to monitor compliance with established policies and procedures of NIDA-Pak. The Policy is not intended to prescribe step by step guidance for every eventuality, but defines the policies which should support the procedures.

Effective date

The Policy will be considered effective from the 1st January 2025 duly approved by the Board of Directors. The Policy is come into force at once, and do hereby supersede any existing Policy.
