Geographic Coverage – NIDA’s Programme Area:

Presently the geographic coverage of NIDA’s projects and programmes include the Urban and Rural Areas of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, FATA, Islamabad Capital Territory, Punjab, Sindh, Baluchistan, AJK, and parts of Gilgit-Baltistan. During the next phase, NIDA intends to significantly expand its coverage and outreach to all districts across the country. The districts will be prioritized for thematic interventions based on the intensity of sufferings, particularly of the women, poor, marginalized and vulnerable populations.
