Objectives

Training on Financial Management Policy

The objective of this training is to ensure all new staff—especially those with programmatic, administrative, or financial responsibilities—understand NIDA-Pakistan’s rules, procedures, and accountability mechanisms for managing funds, and to build a consistent culture of compliance across the organisation.

Promote Compliance & Accountability

  • Familiarize staff with financial policy, donor rules, and statutory requirements.
  • Clarify roles for transparency in financial transactions.

Strengthen Internal Controls

  • Reduce risk of mismanagement, fraud, or misuse.
  • Standardize documentation, authorization, and reporting procedures.

Improve Planning & Reporting

  • Build skills for budgeting, monitoring, and review.
  • Ensure timely, accurate reporting to management and donors.

Efficient Use of Resources

  • Promote cost-effective, prudent financial decision-making.
  • Align resource use with project objectives and impact.

Capacity & Confidence

  • Enable correct application of procedures in daily operations.
  • Clarify delegation of authority and financial limits.

Support Growth & Donor Trust

  • Demonstrate sound stewardship to donors and stakeholders.
  • Maintain compliance across projects to support sustainability.
Speaker notes: Introduce policy context, outline key responsibilities, present 2–3 brief examples of common errors and correct procedures, and finish with where to find policy documents and whom to contact for clarifications.
