Routine pick-and-drop is not provided. For project/field activities transport may be considered (subject to project funds). SMT members with personal vehicles may receive 80–100 liters fuel/month; this allocation will be charged to respective donor projects prorata by budget contribution.

  • Compensation: All pay and rewards within organizational affordability and policy.
  • Increments: Merit-based via PER review.
  • COLA: 5–10% on basic salary once yearly, subject to budget & CEO approval.
  • Recognition Awards: Certificates and/or cash award (Rs.2,000 standard) for exceptional contribution; open to all staff.

31. Office Discipline & Decorum

  • Obey orders and directives from supervisors.
  • Maintain a pleasant, civilized working environment.
  • Use low voice; avoid disturbing colleagues.

Employees should be neat and tidy. Female staff are expected to dress in accordance with cultural norms and professional presentation.

33. Attendance & Punctuality

Upper & Middle Management: Mon–Fri 09:00–17:00 (lunch/prayer break 13:00–14:00).
Support Staff: Mon–Fri 07:00–19:00 (lunch 13:00–14:00). During Ramadan schedules follow government guidance.

  • Regional managers must ensure compliance.
  • Report lateness to supervisor; management may excuse delays in extraordinary situations.
  • Staff must sign attendance register on arrival.

40.3. Types of Sanctions

  • Verbal Warning: First-level reminder; recorded in personal file.
  • Written Warning: Valid 6 months; three within one year may lead to termination.
  • Suspension: Temporary (with/without pay) pending investigation; CEO may suspend up to 4 weeks.
  • Dismissal: For gross misconduct (examples include bribery, serious negligence, fraud, sexual abuse, 10+ days unexplained absence).
