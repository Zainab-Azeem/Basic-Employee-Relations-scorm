Proportionate / Acceptable Risk

Operational activities involve inherent risks. Staff must ensure:

  • The benefit of the activity outweighs the risk.
  • High-risk activities are avoided or mitigated where possible.
  • All safety measures are followed and documented.

Tip: Always assess risk before starting a task and report concerns immediately.
