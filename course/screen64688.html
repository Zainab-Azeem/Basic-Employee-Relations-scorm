Pre-Decisional Involvement (PDI) (continued)

Labor-management forums and pre-decisional involvement allow for:

  • Problem-solving processes to create solutions through consensus decision-making.
  • Collaborative approach, based on parties’ interests rather than their positions.

Consensus decision making and collaboration are alternatives to traditional position-based approaches to labor-management relations.

You can find excellent information, guidance, and training videos about PDI available on the National Council on Federal Labor-Management Relations’ website, www.lmrcouncil.gov. Click on the “PDI” tab at the top of the homepage.
