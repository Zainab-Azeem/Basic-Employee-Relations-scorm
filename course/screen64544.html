Finance and logistics personnel must ensure that the following documents are maintained when processing payments for loading and unloading activities:

  • Labour Attendance Sheet: Record of daily attendance of engaged labourers.
  • Labour Payment Form: Authorized form documenting payment details to labour.
  • ID Cards of Labourers: Verified identification copies of all labourers engaged.
  • Work Done Report / Distribution List: Evidence of tasks completed or goods handled.
  • Waybills / Tally Sheets: Supporting transport documents verifying loading/unloading activities.
