Grievance Procedure

Most grievance procedures involve different levels or steps outlined in the CBA.

The basic steps in grievance procedures include:

  • Informal filing with supervisor (often can be oral)
  • Additional filings at different steps with higher-level managers
  • Arbitration initiated by the union or the agency (can be "appealed")

Ideally, problems are handled and resolved at the informal level.

It is essential that you read and understand the grievance procedure that covers the bargaining unit employees that you support as an ER or ELR specialist. If you have questions concerning that procedure, consult with your assigned or servicing Labor Relations Specialist counterpart.
