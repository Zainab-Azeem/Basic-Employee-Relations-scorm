Official Time

Official time is described in the Section 7131 of the Statute. Official time is paid time granted to employee representatives by the agency to perform representational functions, without charge to leave or loss of pay, when the employee would otherwise be in a duty status.

Official time must be granted to union representatives when engaged in collective bargaining, to attend impasse proceedings, or, as determined by the FLRA, when appearing in any phase of proceedings before the FLRA.
