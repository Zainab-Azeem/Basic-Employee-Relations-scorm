NIDA Strategy 2024-2030

The 2024–30 Strategic Plan positions NIDA-Pakistan to leverage political, economic, social, technological, ecological, and legal opportunities while proactively mitigating related risks — ensuring continued impact, relevance, and resilience.

Goal of the Strategy 2024–2030

NIDA’s goal is: “To reduce poverty, and achieve equitable, climate-resilient, and sustainable improvements in the social and economic conditions of women, poor, and vulnerable communities.”

Theory of Change

NIDA’s theory of change is grounded in the belief that sustainable development can be achieved by building local human capabilities and leveraging multi-sectoral holistic programming through strong partnerships. By focusing on:

  • Social Development
  • Food & Nutrition Security and Economic Development
  • Climate Change Adaptation & Resilience Building
  • Policy Engagement & Advocacy

NIDA aims to reduce poverty and foster equitable, climate-resilient, and sustainable improvements in the social and economic conditions of women, poor, and vulnerable communities. This is achieved through a multi-pronged approach that includes:

  • Strengthening community-driven social sector development
  • Fostering institutional development
  • Improving access to healthcare and quality education
  • Preserving cultural heritage
  • Poverty alleviation and social inclusion of persons with disabilities, youth, and women
