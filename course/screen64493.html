6. Classification of Employees

For the purpose of these rules there shall be the following categories of employees:

6.1. Regular/Permanent Employees

NIDA-Pak has established a Senior Management Team (SMT) as its permanent leadership and institutional backbone. Unlike project staff, SMT members are appointed under open-ended agreements, ensuring organizational continuity and long-term strategic direction.

Composition: The SMT comprises the Chief Executive Officer and Directors of Programs, Operations, Finance, HR & Administration, and Liaison & Coordination, or other core positions as approved by the Board of Directors.

Roles & Responsibilities: Provide policy direction, strategic oversight, and organizational representation while safeguarding institutional memory, compliance, and accountability.

Terms & Conditions
  • Appointment & Tenure: Approved by the Board under indefinite agreements, subject to periodic performance reviews.
  • Benefits: Entitled to full organizational benefits, including health insurance, retirement provisions, paid leave, and professional development.
  • Mobility: May be deputed to projects or emergency assignments by Board approval while retaining substantive SMT status.
  • Termination: Three to six months’ notice required by either party, depending on contractual terms and circumstances.
  • Accountability: Directly responsible to the Executive Board; performance evaluated against annual objectives and KPIs.

6.2. Temporary Employees

Temporary employees are hired to work on a casual basis. The need may arise due to short-term workflow fluctuations or specific tasks. Eligibility for NIDA-Pak’s benefits will be discussed and stated in the employment contract at the time of hiring.

6.3.1. Contract/Project Employees

Contract employees are hired under specific terms of employment outlined in a project contract. They are eligible for NIDA-Pak’s benefits if specified in their contract.

6.3.2. Consultants

Consultants are independent contractors. Their terms of engagement with NIDA-Pak are stipulated within their contract/ToR.
