Examples of Sexual Harassment

Some common examples include:

  • Persistent unwelcome flirting or sexual advances.
  • Displaying sexually suggestive material in the workplace.
  • Verbal abuse or derogatory comments related to gender or sexuality.
  • Any form of coercion or intimidation of a sexual nature.
